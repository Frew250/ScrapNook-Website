Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is an NFT?

    NFT stands for Non Fungable Token.
    For our application, NFT's are modern day collectables. We are in essence publishing your little one's art as a digital scrapbook and stamping it with metat data to prove its authenticity. Much like baseball cards, when printed they were worth only what the shop sold them for, but as the players career progressed the value of their card increased. We mint NFT's by inserting pieces of data onto the blockchain and have that data refernce your art

  • What is the future of NFT's?

    NFT's have been around since 2014 but became extremly popular in 2021, with that popularity some NFt's from notable people were selling for millions.
    We are currently in a downturn of this speculative value, however it is giving room for the techonolgy itself to shine. Brand's and band's are using NFT's, artists and entertainers are using them, each in their own unique ways. At Scrapnook we believe our application of this technology is where it shines the most. We dont know where it will lead, but we do know NFT technology wil be in our future, so we want to get a jumps start and we would love you guys to come along for the ride.
