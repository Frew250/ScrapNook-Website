Why Create NFTs?

We are trying to fix the perishable issue of storing your memories in their normal physical forms. You can save these photos on a hard drive; however, these too are fragile and at times troublesome. We are not looking to replace scrapbooking. We are giving the scrapbookers among us the chance to have backup copies of things they have deemed important. We are giving the people, who reluctantly throw out these memories, the chance to save them in a safe, efficient, and pristine way. We believe this service is valuable and we believe that NFTs are in our future. Below are a few scenarios where this new technology supersedes anything else currently available.

  • Saving Pre-school art.

    Imagine sitting with your grandkids and they ask to hear more about their parents. You dig through the dusty cupboards and water-damaged boxes only to find tattered, faded books and pieces of squished paper. You enjoy touching history, but it is so damaged and worn that you know it will not be long until it will fade away. Creating a collection with ScrapNook will allow you to reach for your phone or iPad and at the click of a button, you have access to hundreds of memories in pristine condition. Not only will you have access to this you can pass these onto your grandkids for them to pass on to their kids.
