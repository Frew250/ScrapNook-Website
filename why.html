Why Create NFTs?

We are trying to address two things; the perishable issue of storing your kid’s art and, offering those who are not great at storing these items a chance to save these memories. We are not looking to replace scrapbooking. We have just seen an opportunity where new technology solves these problems and also has significant untapped potential if you chose to engage.

  • Saving Pre-school art senario.

    Imagine sitting with your grandkids and they ask to hear more about their parents. You dig through the dusty cupboards and water-damaged boxes only to find tattered, faded books and pieces of squished paper. You enjoy touching history, but it is so damaged and worn that you know it will not be long until it will fade away. Creating a collection with ScrapNook will allow you to reach for your phone or iPad and at the click of a button, you have access to hundreds of memories in pristine condition. Not only will you have access to this you can pass these onto your grandkids for them to pass on to their kids.

  • Saving a school history senario.

    Imagine your school had a great year! The soccer team won 2 tournaments; the volleyball team finished first in their league. The school band placed second at nationals, a student won a poetry competition. All these are things that should go down in the school’s history for all to see. If we take pictures of the trophies, a photo of the poem, and a snippet of the tune the band played we can turn these into NFTs. We could archive these achievements over the year and have your history published for all to see.
