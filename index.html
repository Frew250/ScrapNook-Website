It's exciting when your little one starts to bring their paintings home, you put them on the fridge then perhaps on the wall, but then what? At ScrapNook we will scan your little one's art, and using the most up to date technology, create a one of a kind art collection.

  • By transforming your littles one artwork into an NFT gallery, it preserves it online and stamps it with an undenyable signature of authenticity.
  • Setting up an online gallery allows you to share your little one's creative expressions with anyone, anywhere around the world.
  • Documenting your little ones work in this way allows you to see the progression of their development. We are happy to add to your little ones gallery for as long as you will let us!
