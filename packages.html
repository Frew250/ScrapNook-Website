One day, when your little one has grown up, you will be able to tell them you caught their first breath of creativity. At ScrapNook we have a unique application of some state of the art technology, to save your little one's work forever. We will do this for you by creating an NFT gallery, here's how it works:

  • What we need from you
    Click Here!

    If you have purchased a package from us, gather your art and email us at pickup@scrapnook.ca to arrange a pick up of your art. If you are a package winner, gather your art, drop it off at your school and us email pickup@scrapnook.com.

  • What we will do for you
    Click Here!

    Your artwork will be scanned with our high resolution scanner, we will assess and optimize the files then write and organize the metadata. After some more technical wizardry, we will mint your artwork into NFTs and set up an online gallery for you to share with friends and family.

Memories that were once forgotten are now forever stored on the blockchain for you to view whenever, forever!
